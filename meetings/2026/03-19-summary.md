## 2026-03-19 Advisory Board meeting

What follows is a brief summary of the topics discussed in the W3C Advisory Board meeting/call on 2026-03-19

attending: Daniel Appelquist, Hidde de Vries, Wei Ding, Emma Fraser, Max Gendler, Tatsuya Igarashi, An Qi (Angel) Li, Theresa O'Connor, Hiroshi Ota, Avneesh Singh, Amy van der Hiel, Song Xu, Brent Zundel

* **Infoshare**
Hidde gave an update on the location of the July AB meeting and Amy gave a Team update including that Ian would attend an upcoming AB to meeting give an update on part of his work on the Structural Evolution TF. Emma noted the Membership TF will ask for AB feedback on the upcoming 2026 Member survey. 

* **Issue review**
Brent led a review of open Issues in the AB repo. Several issues were marked to close. An issue on "appreciating standards work" was highlighted and proposed to transfer to the Membership TF since Team confirmed there was active discussion in the Membership TFs to support such work. Dan also took an action work with the TAG on an issue on implementation experience. 

* **Draft review: AI Position Statement**
Hidde requested formal approval of the position statement on LLMs in standards work so it could be submitted for transition and publication. He noted he'd integrated feedback from GH and a few wording changes from the AB were made. The AB resolved to publish the document with the changes.

* **Readout from March Member Meeting**
The AB reviewed topics discussed at the March Monthly Member meeting. It was noted the first session had good attendance at 33 people but that it would be good to have more suggestions from AC for the next meeting. It was noted that there would be no April Monthly meeting due to overlap with the AC meeting.
